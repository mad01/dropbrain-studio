DropBrain

Your Personal Timeline for Thoughts

AI-Powered Writing Support

Write confidently with on-device Apple Intelligence (iOS 26+). Tap to correct spelling, grammar, and punctuation—all processed locally, no cloud, preserving your natural voice. 25 free corrections per month, unlimited with Premium.

Built with Accessibility in Mind

Created by someone with dyslexia. Includes accessibility features like OpenDyslexic font option, AI sentence correction, adjustable line spacing and contrast, flexible message padding, and a timeline layout that may help users who think non-linearly.

Flexible Timeline Organization

Traditional note apps force hierarchy. DropBrain offers a different approach—Home Timeline for all thoughts in one stream, Groups to organize by project or topic, Threads to connect related ideas. Capture first, organize later (or never).

Customization at the Center

Every brain processes information differently. Customize everything: 5 fonts (System, OpenDyslexic, Fira Code, JetBrains Mono, Lexend), 9 themes (3 free + 6 premium pastels), multiple message styles (bubble, card, minimal), adjustable spacing, and visual density controls.

Your Data, Your Privacy

Data stored in your private iCloud. AI corrections processed on-device only. No servers, no tracking, no access to your notes. Ad-free. Export or delete anytime. You control your data.

Premium Features ($2.99/month)

Unlimited AI corrections (free: 25/month), 6 additional pastel themes, 4 premium fonts (OpenDyslexic, Fira Code, JetBrains Mono, Lexend), custom app icons, and the ability to hide groups from Home. Cancel anytime.

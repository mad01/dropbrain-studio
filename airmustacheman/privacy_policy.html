Privacy Policy for Air Mustache Man

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

Privacy Commitment

Your privacy is our priority. Air Mustache Man is designed with the highest privacy standards:

  • We have ZERO access to your game data
  • All data stays on your device and your iCloud
  • We do not operate servers that store your information
  • Your game progress and scores are completely private

Overview

Air Mustache Man is a fun arcade game where you fly a mustachioed character through obstacles. Privacy and data security are our top priorities. This privacy policy explains how Air Mustache Man handles your information.

Data Collection

Minimal data collection with maximum privacy.

Air Mustache Man does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers. Everything you do in Air Mustache Man stays on your devices and in your private iCloud account. If you make optional contributions, RevenueCat handles subscription management with anonymous data (see Third-Party Services section below).

Your Game Data Storage

Local Storage

  • All your game progress, high scores, and settings are stored locally on your device
  • Local storage is protected by your device's security features
  • Data is isolated in Air Mustache Man's secure app container

iCloud Storage (Optional)

  • If iCloud sync is enabled, your data syncs to your private iCloud account
  • This data syncs across your devices using Apple's iCloud infrastructure
  • We have no servers and cannot access your iCloud data
  • Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple's iCloud security

Types of Data Stored (All Private)

The following data is stored ONLY on your device and optionally in your iCloud:

  • High scores
  • Game progress and statistics
  • Character unlocks
  • Settings preferences (music, sound)

Important: None of this data is accessible to us. It remains private to you.

What We Don't Do

  • ✓ No Personal Data Collection - We do not collect your name, email, or personal information
  • ✓ No Analytics - We do not track how you play or use the app
  • ✓ No Advertising - Ad-free experience
  • ✓ No Third-Party Data Sharing - Your data is never shared with anyone
  • ✓ No Data Mining - We do not analyze your gameplay patterns
  • ✓ No Data Selling - We do not and will never sell user data
  • ✓ No Servers - We don't operate servers that store your information

Third-Party Services

Contribution Management (Optional)

Air Mustache Man uses RevenueCat to manage optional contributions. RevenueCat may collect minimal information necessary to process transactions:

  • Anonymous user IDs (generated by RevenueCat, not linked to your game data)
  • Purchase receipts (handled by Apple's App Store)
  • Contribution/subscription status

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Critical: Your game data, high scores, and progress are never shared with RevenueCat or any third party. Only anonymous contribution status is processed.

Apple App Store

When you download Air Mustache Man from the App Store, Apple may collect information as described in their privacy policy. We have no access to this information.

Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Permissions

iCloud Access (Optional)

  • Why: To sync your game progress across your devices
  • What we access: Only the private container allocated to Air Mustache Man in your iCloud
  • Who sees it: Only you. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

No Other Permissions Required

Air Mustache Man does not request access to:

  • Your location
  • Your camera or photos
  • Your contacts
  • Your microphone
  • Your calendar
  • Any other sensitive data

Data Security

Your game data security is ensured by:

  • Apple's iCloud encryption - All data encrypted in transit and at rest (if iCloud enabled)
  • iOS sandboxing - Air Mustache Man can only access its own data container
  • No network transmission - Your game data never leaves Apple's ecosystem
  • Device-level encryption - Protected by your device passcode/biometrics

Children's Privacy

Air Mustache Man is designed as a family-friendly game suitable for all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not collect any personal information from any user regardless of age. All game data remains private on the user's device and iCloud account.

Your Rights

You have full control over your game data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible to you on your device
  • Delete: Delete the app to remove all local data
  • iCloud: Manage iCloud data through iOS Settings
  • Reset: Reset game progress through app settings (if available)

Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained until you delete the app
  • iCloud data: Retained until you remove it from iCloud
  • We retain: Nothing. We have no data about you.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will:

  1. Update this privacy policy
  2. Update the "Last Updated" date
  3. Make reasonable efforts to notify users through an app update

Our core commitment: your game data remains private and under your control.

International Users

Air Mustache Man is available worldwide. Your data is stored locally on your device and optionally in your iCloud account (stored in Apple data centers according to Apple's policies). We do not transfer, process, or access your data in any location.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Air Mustache Man's privacy practices, please contact us through the App Store.

However, please note: we cannot help with data recovery or access because we don't have access to your data. Your data is on your device and in your iCloud account, which only you control.

Summary - The Bottom Line

  • We do not collect your game data or personal information
  • We do not have access to your scores, progress, or settings
  • We do not track app usage or gameplay patterns
  • We do not sell user data
  • Your game data stays on your device and in your iCloud
  • We do not operate servers that store your information
  • No analytics, no ads, no tracking
  • Anonymous contribution data handled by RevenueCat only (if you contribute)
  • Family friendly, suitable for all ages (4+)

Air Mustache Man is private by design, not just by policy. Your game data is yours alone.

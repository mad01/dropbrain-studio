Air Mustache Man

Fly, Dodge, Survive! Classic Arcade Fun for Everyone

Simple, Addictive Gameplay

Take control of a mustachioed flying hero and navigate through an endless sky filled with challenges. Swipe up to fly higher, swipe down to dive lower. Avoid obstacles, collect bonuses, and survive as long as possible. Easy to learn, challenging to master!

Dynamic Challenges

Navigate through an ever-changing sky with procedurally generated levels. Dodge spikes, mines, towering pillars, dark clouds, lightning storms, and even angry seagulls! Every run is unique and presents new challenges as the difficulty progressively increases.

Collect Bonuses

Grab collectibles scattered throughout the sky to boost your score. Pickles and mustaches give you small boosts, coins and diamonds are worth more, and special items give massive points. But watch out for negative items that can hurt your score!

Unlock Characters

Discover and unlock new characters as you play. Start with the classic Air Mustache Man and expand your roster through gameplay achievements. Each character has a unique style to show off.
